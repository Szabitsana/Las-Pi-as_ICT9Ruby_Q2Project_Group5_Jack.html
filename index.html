Welcome to OB Montessori!

At Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center, each child is nurtured in the holistic method of Dr. Maria Montessori.

Welcome to O.B. Montessori Center, Philippines! We are a progressive pre-K to 12 co-ed school, blending Montessori tradition with innovation to prepare children for life. Our mission is to nurture self-motivated, capable students who can thrive and shape the future. Explore our programs and experience the vibrant community that makes OBMC a unique place to grow.
